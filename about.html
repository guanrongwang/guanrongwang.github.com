About

Hi, I'm Guanrong!

I'm studying Human Computer Interaction (UX Design and Research) at University of Michigan. Previously, I studied Economics (and took many French and Art history classes) for undergrad.

With combined knowledge in business economics and user experience design, I aim to create not only good in-product experience, but also overall good experience that bridges business needs and users needs.

My interests in designing for better experiences stemmed from my experience of designing social service program, during which I used human-centered approach to understand marginalized communities and design for their needs, at the same time getting a taste of endless sunburn and mosquito bites :)
